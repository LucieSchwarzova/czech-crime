About

www.czechcrime.org s a non-for profit, non-governmental initiative of Otevřená společnost, o.p.s.We believe this website to enhance public debate about the nature of gathering and recording of crime data and about the ways of its presentation towards public.

Recently, police publishes monthly statistics in complicated and user-unfriendly excel tables. With Czechcrime.org, crime data become available and understandable to anybody who is interested.

However, the data published at Czechcrime.org are robust and valid (yet unofficial) source of information about crime in Czech republic. The data can be used under BY-NC-SA Creative Commons license.

The institution of Czechcrime.org has been supported by Think Tank Fund program Open Society Foundations.
